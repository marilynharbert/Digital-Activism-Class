Steganographic Photo Cleaner

Choose a photo below and it will prompt you to enter a message, and an optional password. Then click "hide message", and the photo with the message hidden (with modified pixels as well) will appear. To download the photo, right click on the image and select "Save Image As". To reveal the hidden message and check if it worked, click "reveal message". You can optionally enter a password to hide the message with.
